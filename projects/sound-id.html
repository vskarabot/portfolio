Project details

  • Audio processing: Extracts individual clips from larger recordings.
  • Feature extraction: Converts audio files into spectrograms for model input.
  • Dataset managementSplits data into training and validation sets.
  • Classification Predicts specie from new audio clip.

Next steps

The immediate goal is to improve owl species classification performance by expanding the dataset and refining the training process. Planned improvements include:

  • Data expansion: Collect more owl recordings to reduce overfitting.
  • Data augmentation: Apply techniques such as noise addition, pitch shifting, and time stretching to make the model more robust to real-world recordings.
  • Generalization: Once owl classification is stable, extend the model to recognize other bird species and build a more comprehensive bird sound classifier.
