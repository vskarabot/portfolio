FPL Live

Fantasy Premier League tracker

Short Description

This project allows users to track their FPL team. FPL (Fantasy Premier League) is a game where 'managers' from around the world compete with each other by picking a team of Premier League football players and scoring points based on goals scored, assists etc. I wanted to simplify the proccess of checking live points of my team and teams of other players that I wanted to compare to.

Tools Used

  • Vue.js
  • Express.js

Functionalities

The app allows users to:

  • Search team by id, or select it from predefined content creator id's
  • Overview of current gameweek
  • View league rankings with comparison on previous week
  • View current squad and the points it accumulated.
  • Chart of teams progress throughout the season

In the future I'm planning to improve the app with more statistics of footballers and their teams and maybe implement some point predictions.
